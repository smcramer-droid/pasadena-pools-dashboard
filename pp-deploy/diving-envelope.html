Key Rules & Requirements

1. The diving board tip must be located directly above Point A — the reference point for the entire envelope.

2. Point B is the deepest point of the envelope and must maintain its minimum depth across the full A→B→C zone.

3. The floor slope from C → D cannot exceed 1:3 (1 ft vertical per 3 ft horizontal).

4. The floor slope from the shallow end to the deep area transition cannot exceed 1:7.

5. Half-width rule: ½ of the minimum width at each point must be available on each side of the board centerline.

6. The minimum water depth at Point D (shallow end of envelope) is 4'-6" (54 inches).

7. Board height above water is measured from the water surface to the top of the board at the tip.

8. All dimensions are minimums — they must be met or exceeded. Installing a diving board on a pool that does not meet these specs violates manufacturer instructions and ANSI/APSP standards.
